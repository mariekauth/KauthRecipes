Salad Dressing Cup Cakes or Cake

1 cup sugar
1 salad dressing
1 cup water
2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 cup cocoa
2 cup flour
3 teaspoon vanilla


Bake at 350º for 30 minutes (cup cakes)
Bake at 350º for 45 minutes (cake)

Topping

1 stick or 1/2 cup margarine
8 oz cream cheese
1 1/2 cup powdered sugar
1 tablespoon vanilla
