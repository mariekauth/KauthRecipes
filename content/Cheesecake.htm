My Cheesecake

1 (6 oz) Graham Cracker Crust
1 (8 0z) Package Cream cheese (softened)
1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk
1/3 cup reconstituted lemon juice
1 (21 oz) can cherry pie filling chilled

In medium bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy. Add condensed milk; mix thoroughly. Stir in lemon juice and vanilla. Pour into crust. Chill 2 hours. top with cherry pie filling before serving. Refrigerate. Lid can be used to cover pie and store.
